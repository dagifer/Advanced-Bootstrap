About Me

I'm Dayana Daviott, a nature-loving adventurer with a passion for yoga, climbing, and hiking in the mountains. The great outdoors is where I find my bliss, pushing my limits and immersing myself in the beauty of nature. Fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, I have a knack for connecting with people from diverse backgrounds and bridging cultural gaps. This multilingual ability allows me to forge meaningful connections and embrace the richness of different cultures.


Although I graduated in Tourism Business Administration, my real passion lies in web development. I have chosen to pursue a career in web development at SheCodes.com. This decision reflects my thirst for knowledge and personal growth. By combining my expertise in the tourism industry with my programming skills, I aim to create innovative solutions that enhance user experiences and push the boundaries of what's possible.


In summary, I'm an adventurous spirit who finds joy in the great outdoors and has chosen web development as a path to combine my passions and create meaningful change. With a deep appreciation for nature, a multicultural perspective, and a love for coding, I'm excited to embark on a journey of exploration and growth in the world of web development.
